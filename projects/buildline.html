Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Buildline

How does Buildline differ from GitHub Actions or CircleCI?

Buildline lets you bring your own compute. Instead of renting expensive cloud runners, you connect your own infrastructure‚Äîcloud, on-prem, or hybrid. This means lower costs, better performance, and complete control over your data.

Can I use Buildline with my existing cloud infrastructure?

Absolutely! Buildline works seamlessly with AWS, GCP, Azure, and any Kubernetes cluster. You can also mix cloud and on-premises infrastructure in the same pipeline.

Is Buildline secure for enterprise use?

Yes! Your code never leaves your infrastructure. Agents connect outbound via WebSocket with no inbound ports required. All execution happens in your VPC or on-premises environment.
